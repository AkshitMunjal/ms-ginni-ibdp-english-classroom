04 — Readings

Literary
Theories

Core literary theory texts and themes used to deepen interpretation and comparative thinking.

01

Feminist Theory

Examining voice, agency, and representation through the lens of gender. Students question power structures embedded in canonical texts.

02

Marxist Theory

Analysing ideology, class, and economic structures in literature to reveal how texts both reflect and challenge societal hierarchies.

03

Postcolonial Theory

Interrogating colonial narratives, identity, and cultural hybridity. Students learn to read texts from and about formerly colonised spaces with critical nuance.

These readings help students question voice, power, representation, and ideology while building more sophisticated analytical responses.
